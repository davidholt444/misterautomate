LinkedIn Experience

Your feed, shaped around people you actually care about.

A personal browser extension that quietly reshapes your LinkedIn experience — surfacing the voices that matter to you and stepping back from the rest.

LinkedIn is a great network. The feed just doesn't always reflect why you're there. This extension lets you tune it to feel more like yours.

Download the extension

What it does

This is a lightweight browser extension for Chrome or Edge. It runs entirely on your machine — no accounts, no servers, nothing sent anywhere. It gently reshapes what you see in your feed by deprioritizing content that isn't from people you've chosen to follow.

  • Surfaces posts from connections you actually follow
  • Steps back from suggested and promoted content
  • Reduces visibility of activity from people outside your focus
  • Runs locally — your data stays yours

Should you trust a random browser extension?

Fair question. Browser extensions run with elevated permissions and you have no reason to take a stranger's word for it.

So don't. Verify it yourself.

Download the zip, then upload it directly to your favorite AI — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, whatever you use — and ask it to review the code. Something like:

"I'm about to install this browser extension. Can you read through all the files and tell me exactly what it does, whether it's safe to install, and whether there's anything suspicious or malicious?"

Every line of code in the extension is readable plain text. An LLM can review the entire thing in seconds and give you a straight answer. If anything looks off, don't install it.

A note on use

This extension modifies what's displayed in your browser — it doesn't interact with LinkedIn's servers or your account in any way. That said, tools that alter platform behavior can sometimes sit in a grey area relative to a platform's terms of service.

Use it at your own discretion. It's a personal tool shared as-is, with no guarantees and no warranty. If you're unsure, the Trust section above explains how to have an AI read through the code before you install anything.

Installation

  1. Download and unzip the file above.
  2. Open Chrome or Edge and go to chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions).
  3. Enable Developer mode using the toggle in the top right.
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.
  5. Navigate to LinkedIn — the filter will activate automatically.
